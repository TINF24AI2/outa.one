# Sprint Ceremonies

## Timeline Overview

```
10:30           ~11:00        ~11:15                    12:00
  |                |              |                        |
  |   REVIEW       |    RETRO     |       PLANNING         |
  |   (30 min)     |   (15 min)   |       (45 min)         |
  +----------------+--------------+------------------------+
```

---

## 1. Review (10:30 – ~11:00) · 30 minutes

**Attendees:** Team + Stakeholders (including customer)

**Purpose:** Inspect the past increment and adapt the Product Backlog if needed.

**Agenda:**
- **Demo** – Show what was built this sprint to the customer
- **What was done?** – Walk through completed work 
- **Issues** – Discuss open issues, blockers, and customer feedback

> The customer is present here. Keep the demo focused and honest. No half-finished work.

---

## 2. Retrospective (Retro) (~11:00 – ~11:15) · 15 minutes

**Attendees:**  **Team Only** (no external stakeholders)

**Purpose:** Inspect how the team worked and identify improvements.

**Keep it tight – 15 to 30 minutes max:**
- What went well?
- What needs to improve?
- Action items for next sprint

---

## 3. Sprint Planning (~11:15 – 12:00) · ~45 minutes

**Attendees:** Team

**Purpose:** Define what can be delivered in the next sprint and how.

**Flow:**
```
Product Backlog (PB)  →  Sprint Backlog (SB)
                      |
                  Estimation
                    Assign
                  Sub-Issues
```

**Steps:**
1. **PB → SB** – Pull items from the Product Backlog into the Sprint Backlog
2. **Estimation** – Estimate effort for each item
3. **Assign** – Assign ownership to team members
4. **Sub-Issues** – Break down larger items into actionable sub-tasks
5. **Sprint Goal** – Agree on the single overarching goal for the sprint
